This app is a spin off of AntennaPod, a fully featured Podcast Catcher which helps you to organize, download and listen to all the podcasts you want to. Original author of AntennaPod and first version of Einschlafen Podcast App is Daniel Oeh. Producer of the Einschlafen Podcast, idea for this single purpose app and contributor to consecutive versions is Toby Baier.
